Sir N. R. Narayana Murthy
Founder of Infosys

In the realm of visionary leaders who have left an indelible mark on the world, N.R. Narayana Murthy's name shines brightly. A man of remarkable intellect, unwavering principles, and unparalleled dedication, he, along with his wife Sudha Murthy, has transformed the landscape of technology, philanthropy, and social responsibility. This tribute honors the life and achievements of N.R. Narayana Murthy, a true luminary whose influence extends far beyond the boundaries of the corporate world.

Early Life and Founding Infosys

Born on August 20, 1946, in Karnataka, India, N.R. Narayana Murthy's journey began with a humble upbringing. He completed his education in electrical engineering and went on to establish Infosys in 1981, alongside a group of like-minded individuals. Their audacious vision was to build a software services company that would not only provide top-tier IT solutions but also uphold the highest ethical standards.

Revolutionizing the IT Industry:

Under Murthy's visionary leadership, Infosys became a global tech powerhouse. His emphasis on quality, transparency, and client satisfaction set a new benchmark for the IT industry. Infosys not only contributed significantly to India's economic growth but also played a pivotal role in showcasing the nation's technological prowess on the global stage.

Values and Ethics:

N.R. Narayana Murthy's commitment to ethical business practices and corporate governance remains an enduring legacy. He emphasized the importance of maintaining integrity, fairness, and transparency in all dealings, inspiring a generation of entrepreneurs to prioritize values over short-term gains. His principled approach earned Infosys the respect and trust of clients and stakeholders alike.

Social Responsibility:

Beyond his professional achievements, Murthy recognized the responsibility of the privileged to give back to society. Alongside his wife Sudha Murthy, he established the Infosys Foundation, a philanthropic organization committed to driving positive change in healthcare, education, rural development, and more. Their unwavering dedication to social causes has transformed countless lives and communities across India.

Leadership and Mentorship:

N.R. Narayana Murthy's leadership style was marked by his ability to empower and inspire those around him. He believed in fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning within Infosys. His mentorship has shaped the careers of numerous professionals, cultivating a new generation of leaders who share his values.

Awards and Recognition:

In 2014, Mr. Murthy was ranked 13th among CNBC’s 25 global business leaders and listed among the ‘12 greatest entrepreneurs of our time’, by Fortune in 2012. He has received Legion d’honneur from France, CBE from Britain, and Padma Vibhushan from India. The Economist ranked him among the 10 most-admired global business leaders in 2005. Mr. Murthy is ranked among the top 10 Financial Times’ list of “Business pioneers in technology”, published in March 2015. He is the first Indian winner of Ernst and Young’s World Entrepreneur of the Year award..
